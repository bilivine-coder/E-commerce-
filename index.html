The Challenge
The $4.5M leaky bucket
Nordic Knots has a brand people love and a product worth $500 a pop. Traffic is strong. The site is beautiful. But behind the polished storefront sits a gaping hole — 70% of carts are abandoned every month. That's 1,750 people who clicked "Add to Cart" and disappeared. Plus 1,200 email subscribers who have never made a single purchase. Nearly 40,000 lost opportunities per month — people who raised their hand and said "I want this" but were never followed up with.
At $500 AOV, that silence costs Nordic Knots $190,000 every 30 days. Annually: over $2.1M walking out the door without a receipt.
The Solution
The 24/7 recovery machine
1
Smart Email Recovery Flow — Triggers within 1 hour of abandonment with dynamic product images and a one-click return-to-cart. Converts the easiest wins first.
2
SMS / WhatsApp Layer — For high-intent carts over $500, a gentle text nudge delivers 3× higher open rates than email alone.
3
Inactive Lead Reactivation — A separate drip turns 1,200 dormant email signups into first-time buyers using best-sellers and live social proof.
